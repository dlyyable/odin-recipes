Recipe: Americano

Nothing beats a good ol' cup of coffee first thing in the morning!

Ingredients:

  • 20g coffee beans
  • 1 coffee grinder
  • 1 espresso machine
  • 1 portafilter
  • 150ml water
  • 200g ice

Steps

  1. Grind the coffee beans using the coffee grinder to fineness of your liking
  2. Transfer the grinded beans to your espresso machine
  3. Extract the espresso (prefertably within 27-32 seconds range)
  4. Pour extracted espresso into a cup
  5. Add 150ml water and ice
